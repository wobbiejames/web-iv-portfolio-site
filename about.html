About Me

Hello! My name is Robert Taylor and I am an emerging designer based in Lincoln, Nebraska. I am a recent graduate from the Graphic Design | Media Arts program at Southeast Community College and through my education I have studied modern and historical design theory and practices. While I am proud of what I have done with this knowledge as a designer, I know that design never stops, and I am eager to continue learning and growing!

As a designer, I love building pieces from the ground up and find that I am most passionate about projects that I can leave a personal touch on. I enjoy working in print, and think Design should be accessible to anyone. As a designer I want to make work that is relatable and engaging.

Please go check out my work here, and see what I have to offer and a designer! If you have any questions, feel free to get in touch here!
